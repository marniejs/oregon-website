Salem

Salem is the capital city of Oregon and is located in the northwest region of the state. Classified as urban3, the population of Salem is approximately 177,487 according to the United States Census Bureau1. The city was incorporated in 18572. The median income is $62,1851, which is lower than the state's overal median income.
