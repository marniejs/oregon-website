Salem

Salem is the capital city of Oregon. The population of Salem is approximately 177,487 according to the United States Census Bureau (1). Salem was incorporated in 1857 (2). It is located in the northwest region of Oregon. The city's classification is urban (3). The median income is $62,185 (1), which is lower than the state's overal median income.
